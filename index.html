About

EYE TRACKING

We use innovative and reliable Eye Tracking technology to enable everyone to control our programs. Physical interaktion with Mouse or Keyboard are not necessery which enables physical disabled people to use the Program.

3D-MODEL

Easy to learn eye movements allow the user to attach cubes to each other and build an endless amount of models. The models can illustrate single objects, big enviroments and everything abstract your mind can come up with!

SAVING

All models can be saved as Screenshots with diverse Backgrounds and shared over social media. Another way to save your project is by exporting the object to a 3D-Printer and bring it into the physical world.
